Privacy Policy

Support Privacy Policy

The short version

Dolphin Dash does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The only thing saved is your high score, and it stays on your device.

What we collect

Your current high score is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults storage. It is never transmitted to us, to Apple, or to any third party — it simply lets the game remember your best run between sessions.

We do not collect your name, location, contacts, photos, or any other personal data, and Dolphin Dash does not require an account or sign-in.

Third-party services

As of this policy's last update, Dolphin Dash does not use any analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking SDKs. If that changes in a future update, we'll update this page and the app's listing to reflect it.

Children's privacy

Dolphin Dash is suitable for players of all ages. Because the game does not collect any personal information from any user, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll post the update here with a new "last updated" date below.
