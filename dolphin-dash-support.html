Troubleshooting

My high score disappeared

High scores are saved locally on your device. Reinstalling the app or switching to a new device will reset it — there's currently no cloud sync.

I don't hear any sound

Check that your device isn't on silent/mute and that in-game sound is turned on from the main menu.

The game crashed or froze

Try closing and reopening the app first. If it keeps happening, email us with your device model and iOS version so we can look into it.
